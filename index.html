Akhil Joshua Dennison

I like figuring out what a business is really worth — and arguing about what it should do next.

I'm a B.Com (Financial Technology) graduate from Vellore Institute of Technology, where I majored in Finance. Somewhere between spreadsheets and case competitions, I discovered that what I actually enjoy is sitting with a company's numbers until they start telling a coherent story - and then making the case for what that story means in a succint action plan.

Outside of that, I'm the person who'll happily debate you about almost anything — I did it competitively at university, and honestly never really stopped. I read and write about any topic under the sun, play tennis with more enthusiasm than skill, and have a long-running fascination with cars and how they're engineered. I take my coffee seriously enough to have opinions about it.As my favourite author JRR Tolkien said "Still round the corner there may wait; A new road or a secret gate..."; I try to keep myself open to opportunities and I welcome challenges with the same spirit.
